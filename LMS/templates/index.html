Library Portal

Library Management made easier.

As long as an Internet connection is available, our Library is accessible anywhere and at any moment using a simple technological device, such as a PC, a tablet or even a smartphone. This means students can take care of all their library needs without having to wait and go to the nearest physical library. They can do it in a formal environment, for example at school, or they can relax at their homes getting an instant access to the information they need.

Working of a Traditional Library

An online library management system essentially comprises of all the facilities a library provides and a lot more. It comprises of a large number of books, respective authors and any vital information related to it. It also keeps a record of the students issuing the books, reissing them, fines charged etc. Individuals working for the library are also given special rights.

Our Product

We have created a website that organises various library elements for optimal functioning of the same. In addition to the traditional library portal database, the provision of an online buying/selling service of books has also been catered to.
